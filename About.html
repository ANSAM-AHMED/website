About Us

[OxGreen] Agricultural Equipment Company.

[OxGreen] was founded to meet the needs of farmers and agricultural producers by offering a comprehensive range of high-quality agricultural equipment. We work to provide the latest technologies and equipment that contribute to improving the efficiency of agricultural production and increasing returns

Our products include: 1- Plowing and agricultural machines: such as plows and cultivators. Irrigation devices: drip and sprinkler irrigation systems. 2- Harvesting equipment: Advanced and efficient harvesting machines. 3- Plant care tools: from pesticides and fertilizers to spraying equipment. At [OxGreen] we are committed to providing outstanding customer service and ongoing technical support, in addition to agricultural consultations to ensure the success of our customers. Our goal is to promote sustainable development in the agricultural sector and achieve the best results for farmers. Join us on our journey towards better agriculture!
